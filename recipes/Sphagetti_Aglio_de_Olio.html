Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a traditional Italian pasta dish from Naples. It is made with just a few simple ingredients: spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. This dish is quick to prepare and is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a late-night snack.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • Â½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, add olive oil and sliced garlic. Cook until the garlic is golden brown, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the garlic oil. If the pasta seems dry, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until desired consistency is reached.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately.

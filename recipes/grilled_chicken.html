Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken

Description

This Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken recipe is a delicious and easy way to prepare chicken on the grill. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper, which infuses it with flavor and helps keep it moist and tender as it cooks. The chicken is then grilled until it is golden brown and cooked through, resulting in a juicy and flavorful dish that is perfect for a summer barbecue or a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Stir in rosemary, garlic, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant. Remove from heat, and let cool.
  2. Place chicken in a shallow baking dish. Rub with salt and pepper. Pour rosemary mixture over chicken, and turn to coat. Cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil grate.
  4. Grill chicken for 6 to 8 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear when pierced with a fork.
